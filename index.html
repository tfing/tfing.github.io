Note of Option

Jun 18, 2024
What’s Option 🔗Option is a contract that derivated from the underlying stock. It gives the contract buyer the right to buy or sell the stock at a certain price before a certain date. the certain price is called ‘strike price’. the certin date is called ’expiration date’. If user A buys a contract with the right to buy the stock from user B, user A can decide when to execute the contract before expiration date.
